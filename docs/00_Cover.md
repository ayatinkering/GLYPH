# Cover

GLYPH is not a fitness app.

It is not a walking tracker.

It is not another AI art generator.

It is not Strava.

It is not Spotify Wrapped.

It is not GitHub.

GLYPH introduces an entirely new digital artifact.

The **Mandala Commit**.

Every walk outside becomes a deterministic mathematical composition generated entirely from human movement.

Your footsteps become geometry.

Your gait becomes rhythm.

The sky becomes atmosphere.

The result is a computational artifact that could only have been created by that exact walk.

Not because AI imagined it.

Because mathematics reconstructed it.

# Why GLYPH Exists

Developers spend thousands of hours creating digital artifacts.

Repositories.

Commits.

Applications.

Websites.

Design systems.

Yet one of the most valuable activities for creativity—

walking—

leaves behind nothing.

A beautiful walk disappears.

A peaceful sunset disappears.

The rhythm of your footsteps disappears.

GLYPH asks a simple question:

> **What if movement deserved its own medium?**

Photography preserved light.

Audio recording preserved sound.

Video preserved time.

Git preserved code.

GLYPH preserves movement.

# Product Manifesto

We believe movement is creative.

We believe mathematics is beautiful.

We believe software should encourage people to step away from software.

We believe walking should produce something more meaningful than calories burned.

We believe algorithms can reveal beauty without generating fake imagery.

We believe computational design is an artistic medium.

We believe the most beautiful interface is often the quietest one.

We believe developers should touch grass.

---

# What Is A Mandala Commit?

A Mandala Commit is the permanent computational record of one walk.

It contains

- a mathematically generated mandala
- a procedural rhythm generated from gait
- metadata describing the mathematics
- a numbered artifact
- an optional GitHub contribution

A Mandala Commit is to movement what a Git commit is to software.

# Product Principles

### Mathematics over AI

Every pixel should be explainable.

Nothing should be hallucinated.

The artwork is generated through algorithms, not prompts.

---

### Motion over Metrics

The goal is not counting steps.

The goal is transforming movement into art.

---

### Beauty over Features

Remove unnecessary functionality.

Every feature must support the central idea.

---

### Calm over Complexity

Whitespace is a feature.

Silence is a feature.

The artwork should always remain the hero.

---

### Craft over Novelty

Use timeless interaction design.

Avoid trends.

The interface should still feel elegant years from now.
